Northwinds Apartments

These are luxurious fully furnished 2BHK and 3BHK Serviced Apartments, located in the fast-developing professional hub of India – North Bangalore. This is one of the most preferable locations near the Kempegowda International Airport. It offers you a warm welcome stay with air-conditioned rooms, elevators, Wi-fi connectivity and power backup. The apartments have a huge living/dining room, two/three bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms, kitchen area, and balconies. The apartments are scrupulously maintained with a round-the-clock attendant.

We ensure a daily linen change and also offer a laundry service. The apartment has LCD TV with Cable/Tata Sky connectivity for you to unwind and relax after a long day. We provide all meals, with menus specially prepared by our expert multi-cuisine chefs, keeping in mind the culinary needs of our guests, set by the management. Well-trained and friendly staff, attractive amenities and a competitive pricing is what makes us stand out from other serviced apartments. To ensure safety and security, CCTV covers all floors and premises. Security is further heightened keeping in view the COVID-19 situation. All staff are on campus and trained to ensure COVID safety norms of service, safety and hygiene are maintained at all times.
