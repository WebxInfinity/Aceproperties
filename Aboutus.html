Company information

Ace Property Services aims to provide corporate clients an alternative to expensive Hotel accommodation, yet offering quality with emphasis on a personal level of service, with a homely feel. We have professional management at the helm and a team of dedicated staff on site, who take pride in delivery of service. All my Serviced Apartments and Company Guest Houses are provided with either 2 or 3 (en-suite) bedrooms, with the comfort and ambiance you would expect for your employees and guests. They epitomise comfort, style and luxury. We believe in building long-standing relationships with all our clients, which is why we work on an ongoing improvement process for the services we offer. Keeping in tune with the trying times the country and the world is going through with COVID 19, we have stayed abreast to ensure safety of staff and clients. We adhere strictly to following all COVID safety protocols as recommended by the government.
